Wings Brigade is the primary brigade for all aviation and air assault-related matters inside 101st. This brigade ensures the safety of the United States of America and The United States Army from the air as it holds all of the pilots of the 101st Division.

Why Wings?

With over 7000 flights, Wings is the most experienced and elitest aerial brigade in all of USAR, and is the only brigade that can provide reliable and precise heavy air support.

Transportation

Wings Brigade stands as the most experienced and elite aerial transport unit in USAR. From rapid troop deployments and precision drop-offs to life-saving medevacs and cargo transport, Wings ensures fast, reliable, and efficient air mobility in any operation.

Aerial Support

With unmatched precision and firepower, Wings Brigade dominates the skies, providing the most reliable and devastating aerial support in USAR. Whether it's close air support, reconnaissance, or escort missions, Wings ensures battlefield superiority from above.

Schooling

Wings Brigade sets the standard for elite pilot training in USAR. Through rigorous training and hands-on flight experience, we develop highly skilled and disciplined aviators, prepared for any mission with confidence and precision.

Pilot Qualifications Company

Pilot Qualifications Company, also known as the Pilot Qualifications School is the entrance Company of Wings. The Company itself is split into two, you have the school and the actual company, which serves different purposes. The school examines and teaches Pilot candidates the basic necessities they will need to be a successful pilot, and the company holds Pilot Instructors which hosts and runs the aforementioned school.
